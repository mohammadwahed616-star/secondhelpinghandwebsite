Who We Are

Building a Better
Tomorrow, Together

Second Helping Hands Foundation is a charitable and religious NGO committed to the holistic welfare of society. We believe every person deserves dignity, nourishment, education, and opportunity.

From distributing food to the hungry, to constructing Masjids and Madarshas, to training youth with life skills — our work spans the full spectrum of community development, driven by the spirit of Sadaqah and service.

What We Do

Our Programs & Initiatives

Eight pillars of service, united by one mission — to serve humanity with sincerity.

🍱

Food Distribution

Providing nutritious meals to families living in poverty, the homeless, and those affected by economic hardship — no one should sleep hungry.

Poverty Alleviation

Holistic support programs that address the root causes of poverty — from direct financial aid to long-term livelihood creation.

Long-term Support

Skill Development

Vocational training, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship workshops empowering youth and adults to become self-sufficient.

Youth & Adults

Natural Disaster Relief

Rapid emergency response teams deployed during floods, earthquakes, and other calamities — delivering relief kits, food, and shelter.

Health Support

Free medical camps, medicine drives, and health awareness programs bringing quality healthcare to remote and underserved communities.

Masjid Construction

Building and renovating Masjids in underserved areas so communities have a dedicated sacred space for worship and gathering.

Religious Infrastructure
